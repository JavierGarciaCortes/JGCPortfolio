This project Hotel del Oso I had to redo the website for a Hotel. I make a new design for the website of the hotel and also made some improvements in the back-end.

Firstly I have implemented a section so that workers can add new customers, look for the IDs of existing customers, make reservations and modify or delete them once they have been made.

In the case of adding a new client, first filter the data entered so that they do not enter HTML code. Also check that the photo we upload is correct (size and format), after that assigns it a name (customer name - ID number .jpg).

To reserve the room, first check if the client ID exists. Then, check that the date of entry is not earlier than the current day. Also that the departure day is not prior to the entry day. And finally, that on the selected dates there is no reservation for that type of room.

Once the reservation is made. It is possible to modify the dates of entry and exit, the type of room or delete the reservation.

Hotel workers can also see a list of all reservations. They can see it complete, or paginated in 3, 4 or 5 reservations per page.

The clients, in the section requesting a reservation, will find a form to request a reservation and a table with the prices of the rooms. And below is the access form for workers.

When the client requests a reservation, all fields are mandatory, except for additional requests. If you have forgotten any when submitting the form, notify us of the missing field and fill in the rest of the fields with the values we had set. To avoid having to fill out the entire form again.

Once all the fields written by the customer have been filtered, send an email to the hotel and the customer with the reservation details. It also gives us an on-screen confirmation message of the email or error

Finally, in the reservation zone section, customers with their first and last name can see a list of their reservations.

This project I make it with Bootstrap for the fornt-end and PHP for the back-end. It have responsive design.
