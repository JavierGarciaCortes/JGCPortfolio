This project Jeongye's Academy is a website for a language Academy. The main page is ad about the classes, a small introduction of the teacher and introduction of the topic about the blog.

The blog is about korean food and places in South Korea. In which the people can leave their comments on the posts.

It also has the student zone, where the academy students can download the material for the class and chat with other studients. Also login with the academy id, the academy can upload posts, manage people's comments, control the assistance of the students, chechk/modify the information of the ald and new students, control de financial of the academy, etc.

This project I made it with HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, Javascript, Ajax and PHP. It have responsive design. And you can chose between five diferent lenguages
